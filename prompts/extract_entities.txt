[P1 dien] Trich entity tu mot Dieu/Khoan/Diem.

Input: node_id + text goc.
Output JSON theo schema ExtractedEntities:
  subjects[], obligations[], rights[], prohibitions[],
  penalties[{type, min_amount, max_amount, duration_months, is_permanent, text}],
  deadlines[], references[]

QUY TAC:
- Chi trich cai CO trong text. Khong suy dien, khong bo sung kien thuc ngoai.
- Khong co loai nao -> tra mang rong, dung bia.
- Muc phat ghi bang so VND. "tuoc bang lai 2 den 4 thang" -> duration_months = 4, is_permanent = false.
